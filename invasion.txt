- Bombardment
  - Choose 1 planet to use Bombardment ability on
  - Defender destroys ground forces according to hits
- Commit Ground Forces
  - Active player lands Infantry from their ships to the planets they wish to invade
  - Can split forces to separate planets
- Space Cannon Defense
  - PDS on planet may fire on invaders before landing
- Ground Combat
  - Resolve ground combat following the same Combat Rolls and Assign Hits rules for space combat
  - Retreat is NOT possible at this point for either player
- Establish Control
  - Active player gains control of planet if at least 1 of their units remain
  - Gain corresponding Planet card from supply/player, and exhaust
  - Remaining enemy structures (ie PDS, Space Docks) are destroyed
